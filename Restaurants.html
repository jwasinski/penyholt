Pubs and Restaurants In Pembrokeshire

There are many restaurants and great family pubs scattered throughout Pembrokeshire. Below is just a small sample of them.

Stackpole Inn, Stackpole – 01646 672324
An award winning restaurant voted “Best food experience in Pembrokeshire 2011″.

The Carew Inn, Tenby – 01646 651267
A long established family run pub with a great reputation for its home cooked food.

St Govans Inn, Bosherton – 01646 661311
A pub and restaurant very close to the beautiful Bosherton Lily ponds.

Ocean Restaurant, Tenby – 01834 844536
A high end restaurant which uses only the freshest local ingredients. Booking recommended.

The Mermaid, Saundersfoot – 01834 811873
A restaurant that specializes in freshly caught seafood and traditional home cooking.

Martha’s Vineyard, Milford Haven – 01646 697083
A well establish restaurant and cafe/bar overlooking the Milford Haven marina.

The Blue Ball, Tenby – 01834 843038
A restaurant that locally sources all of its ingredients and offers great seafood.
